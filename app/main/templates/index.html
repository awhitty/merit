Something special

National Honor Society is a nationally recognized organization that commends students for outstanding scholarship, citizenship, service, and character. Cherry Creek NHS members participate in a wide variety of tutoring and community service opportunities in the school and community.

Rewarding excellence



We want to reward the hard work and diligence students have devoted to their academic careers. We also want to build a community of the most talented and motivated minds at Creek. To achieve these goals, we use a three-step application process to find the students we think best align with our values.

The Process

1 Invitation

Qualifying students are invited to apply for the next year's NHS chapter typically about a month into the second semester.

2 Application

In order to be considered for induction into the society, invitees must submit an application answering a short essay prompt and a few questions.

3 Selection

Once applications are submitted, the executive board will read through each application before selecting a handful of welcome inductees.
